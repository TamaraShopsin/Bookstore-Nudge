Who is this page for?
Sites that want to link to local bookstores rather than just online retailers.

How does it work?
It really just uses google maps.

What do I do?
Choose between two pages:

1. geolocation.html
Uses HTML 5 geolocation to automatically get a users zipcode.

or

2. searchtop.html
Uses a traditional search box to ask for the zipcode.


Then link to either of the pages.

or

Download the source code and adapt it to your site.
